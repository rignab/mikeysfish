About Mikey's Fish

🎯

Our Mission

To provide the freshest, highest-quality salmon to our community while supporting sustainable fishing practices and local fishermen.

We believe that everyone deserves access to premium salmon that's not only delicious but also responsibly sourced. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do.

🌊

How We Source Our Fish

🎣

Direct from Fishermen

We work directly with local fishermen who share our commitment to sustainable practices and quality.

❄️

Fresh Daily

Our salmon is caught fresh every morning and delivered to our shop the same day, never frozen.

🌱

Sustainable Practices

We only source from fisheries that follow sustainable fishing methods and environmental regulations.

Our Quality Promise

Every piece of salmon that leaves our shop is guaranteed to be fresh, properly handled, and of the highest quality. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll make it right.
