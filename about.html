About Us

ʔi čəxʷ and Welcome to Mikey and Myla's Fish Co. family! Through the Treaty of Point Elliott of 1855, the Coast Salish tribes of this region (Snohomish, Snoqualmie, Skykomish, Stillaguamish, Duwamish, Suquamish, and others who make up the Tulalip Tribes today)—reserved our rights to fish in our usual and accustomed places. Our fleet of fishermen have generations of experience that taught us to love every catch we bring to shore. That means every salmon carries the story of our people, our rez, and our ancestral connections to the land and sea. We at Mikey's pride ourselves on delivering our customers the freshest, highest-quality salmon straight from the waters we've fished for centuries! Whether it's smoked, flash-frozen, or fresh off the boat, our salmon is handled with care and intention, honoring both tradition and taste. t́igʷicid for supporting us! We hope to see you soon!
